Partnership with Gameday

Industry Challenges

The evolution of season passes has left a gap in the personalized customer experience. Crowding, delayed pass sales, and a declining youth demographic have all contributed to challenges in guest satisfaction and revenue predictability.

Gameday’s Solution

Gameday introduces a cutting-edge, gamified system that transforms the ski resort experience. By integrating an achievement system, point-based rewards, and live feedback, we incentivize exploration and early pass purchases—addressing crowd management, improving guest satisfaction, and stabilizing cash flow.

Unique Value Proposition

  • Proven success in gamified recreational experiences
  • Expansion opportunity to create lifelong customers
  • Ease of integration with existing resort systems

Target Customer Segment

Our primary market focuses on the 13-24 age group—a demographic increasingly receptive to technology and gamified experiences. Through this partnership, we provide tailored solutions for leading corporations such as Vail Resorts, Alterra Mountain Company, Entabeni Systems, and Mountain Collective.

About the App

My Epic App is designed to revolutionize your mountain experience. With an intuitive interface and cutting-edge features, you can manage your ski passes, plan your adventures, and stay updated on resort conditions—all in one place. Our new partnership with Gameday adds a dynamic, gamified layer that enhances engagement, drives early pass purchases, and secures guest loyalty across the network.
